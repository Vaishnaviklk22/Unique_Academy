About Us


Unique Academy

Unique Academy is a premier institute based in Raipur, Chhattisgarh, dedicated to preparing students for UPSC, CGPSC, and other state-level and central civil services examinations. With a strong foundation in academic excellence, result-oriented teaching, and personalized mentorship, we have emerged as a trusted name among civil services aspirants across the state.



Vision & Mission


Our Vision

To empower and guide the youth of Chhattisgarh and beyond towards successful careers in public service, by providing high-quality and affordable education tailored for competitive examinations.


Our Mission

->To deliver comprehensive and updated coaching for UPSC, CGPSC, VYAPAM, SSC, and other competitive exams. ->To cultivate analytical thinking, clarity of concepts, and answer writing skills essential for civil services success. ->To provide accessible education through both online and offline modes to cater to diverse learning needs.
